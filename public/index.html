My favorite baseball player

This is a picture of Shohei Ohtani, MLB player for the Los Angeles dodgers. He is a two-way player who can pitch and hit. He is the most popular baseball player in Japan. I respect him because He's not only a good baseball player, he's a good character. I would like to see his games in the US someday.
